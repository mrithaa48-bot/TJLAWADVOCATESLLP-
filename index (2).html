What We Do

Practice Areas

Our multidisciplinary team provides comprehensive legal support across seven core practice areas, each supported by specialists with deep sector-specific knowledge and a practical, commercially focused approach.

01
Conveyancing & Real Estate Law
From complex development projects to straightforward acquisitions, we bring substantial experience in every stage of a transaction.
  • Land and corporate due diligence
  • Structuring of transactions
  • Acquisition, disposal & transfer of property
  • Residential, commercial & mixed-use developments
  • Construction contracts & joint development agreements
  • Residential, commercial & industrial leases
  • Environmental law regulatory requirements
02
Commercial Law
We support businesses at every stage of their life cycle — supply agreements, manufacturing contracts, software licensing, and more.
  • Business structure & investment framework advisory
  • Government approvals, permits & licences
  • Preparing, negotiating & reviewing commercial agreements
  • Kenyan regulatory regime advisory
03
Corporate Law & Company Secretarial
Sound legal structuring, strong governance frameworks, and full statutory compliance for companies of every size.
  • Structuring & restructuring of corporate entities
  • Corporate governance advisory
  • Due diligence reviews
  • Private equity & venture capital advisory
  • Shareholders' agreements & corporate documentation
  • Competition & regulatory advisory
04
Employment & Labour Law
Practical, mutually beneficial workplace policies aligned with legal requirements and commercial objectives.
  • Employment contract development & review
  • Workplace compliance & employee benefits
  • Compensation & termination of executives
  • Trade union recognition
  • Collective bargaining agreements
  • Employment litigation
05
Dispute Resolution
The highest standard of legal and strategic advice across mediation and litigation, for high-value and complex claims.
  • Comprehensive risk assessment from the outset
  • Commercial litigation
  • Mediation & alternative dispute resolution
  • Construction & real estate disputes
  • Banking & financial services disputes
  • Professional negligence claims
06
Immigration Law
Strategic, solution-driven advice for corporations, investors, professionals, and families navigating immigration processes.
  • Corporate immigration & work permit applications
  • Employment & investor permits
  • Permanent residence applications
  • Citizenship & naturalization
  • Family reunification & dependent passes
  • Immigration compliance audits
07
Succession, Family Law & Estate Planning
Effective succession planning preserves wealth, ensures business continuity, and safeguards family interests across generations.
  • Drafting of wills and codicils
  • Estate and inheritance planning
  • Trust creation and administration
  • Probate and letters of administration
  • Succession planning for family businesses
  • Wealth structuring and asset protection
  • Contentious probate and estate disputes
